How can I get started?

Quick guide to the workspace selector
  • Add a workspace Click "Add Workspace" and enter a name and your Databricks URL (e.g. https://abc-123.cloud.databricks.com).
  • Connect Click "Connect" on any workspace card, or click its name, to launch that workspace.
  • Search Use the search bar to filter workspaces by name or URL.
  • Edit or delete Click the ⋮ menu on a workspace card to edit its details or remove it.
  • Recent The Recent tab shows the workspaces you've connected to most recently.
  • Settings Clear your recent history or delete all saved workspaces from the Settings tab.
